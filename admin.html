How to use
  1. Click Download Excel Template — it includes every employee and each day from 01-06-2026 to 30-06-2026 (date format dd-mm-yyyy).
  2. Fill in Punch In and Punch Out times (e.g. 08:30 or 08:30:00). Sundays are weekly off and are not included in the template.
  3. Upload the completed file below. Rows without a punch-in time are skipped.
  4. Existing records for the same employee and date will be updated.
